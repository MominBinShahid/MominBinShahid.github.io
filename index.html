About Me

My name is Momin Bin Shahid, and I am a dedicated Full-Stack Software Engineer with a deep enthusiasm for software engineering and cutting-edge technologies, particularly in the domain of Web and JavaScript

With a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science and over 5 years of experience in the software development field, complemented by an additional 1+ years in sales and marketing, I possess a profound understanding of the development lifecycle and adhere to industry standards. I thrive on acquiring knowledge about various processes, building innovative solutions, and effectively promoting them to the market.

My expertise lies in creating and maintaining robust solutions utilizing stacks such as MERN , MEVN, MEAN, and JAM. These ventures span a diverse range of domains, including Internet Privacy and Security, Metadata Platforms, Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP), Human Resource Management Systems (HRMS), EdTech (E-learning platform for educational institutions), and Bioinformatics projects with research institutions, among others.

Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of working with both service-based and product-based companies, collaborating with clients hailing from the United States, Europe, the Middle East, and other regions across the globe.

My insatiable curiosity drives me to constantly explore and experiment with emerging technologies and concepts. I am always enthusiastic about developing Web , Mobile , and Desktop applications using JavaScript and other exciting technologies.

In my recent experiences, I have extensively utilized JavaScript-based technologies such as React.js , Vue.js, and Node.js. Furthermore, I have hands-on experience working with On-Premise and Cloud infrastructures such as AWS, Azure, and Firebase (GCP). I have successfully built and maintained monolith, serverless (FaaS), and microservices-based applications, leveraging Docker and Kubernetes to enhance efficiency, scalability, and portability while ensuring seamless deployment (CI/CD) and management.

My primary highlight is that I have a strong passion for solving complex problems and a drive for continuous and quick learning, I consistently strive to stay updated with the latest industry trends and technologies. I have a proven track record of successfully delivering high-quality software solutions that meet and exceed client expectations . My strong analytical skills, attention to detail, and ability to work effectively in cross-functional teams make me a valuable asset in delivering innovative and scalable software solutions.
